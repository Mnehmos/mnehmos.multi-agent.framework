# Research Command

Execute comprehensive research with multi-perspective analysis and knowledge synthesis.

## **Command Options**

### **Research Specification**
- `topic` - Research subject or question
- `--depth=[basic|comprehensive|deep]` - Research depth (default: comprehensive)
- `--sources=[web|academic|patents|industry]` - Source types (default: web,academic)
- `--perspectives=[technical|business|user|competitive]` - Analysis perspectives (default: all)
- `--synthesis=[yes|no]` - Include knowledge synthesis (default: yes)

### **Research Configuration**
- `--methodology=[systematic|exploratory|mixed]` - Research approach (default: systematic)
- `--timeframe=[days|weeks]` - Research duration (default: 2 weeks)
- `--quality=[high|medium|low]` - Source quality threshold (default: high)
- `--language=[english|multilingual]` - Language preference (default: english)

### **Output Configuration**
- `--format=[markdown|json|report]` - Output format (default: markdown)
- `--citations=[yes|no]` - Include citations (default: yes)
- `--summary=[yes|no]` - Include executive summary (default: yes)
- `--recommendations=[yes|no]` - Include recommendations (default: yes)

## **Usage Examples**

```bash
# Comprehensive technical research
/research "microservices architecture" --depth=comprehensive --perspectives=technical,business --sources=web,academic --synthesis=yes

# Market competitive analysis
/research "cloud database providers" --perspectives=competitive --sources=industry,web --timeframe=4weeks

# User experience research
/research "mobile app onboarding" --perspectives=user --depth=basic --recommendations=yes

# Deep academic research
/research "quantum computing applications" --depth=deep --sources=academic,patents --methodology=systematic --citations=yes
```
